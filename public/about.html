Reading
about

This is a read through the Bible in a year program based on the Discipleship Journal Reading Plan. Each day has 4 readings, each from a different section of the Bible (Old Testament, New Testament, Gospel, and Wisdom). At the end of the four readings there's a button to mark that day's reading done, and when you refresh the page or return to the page tomorrow it'll show you the next day's reading. Each month has 25 readings so there are built into the schedule skip/miss days.

One of the goals of this project is to allow for a mobile friendly way of letting you read, when you don't have your Bible in-hand.

At the bottom of the page the current weekly Fighter Verse is presented for memorization.

To avoid making you sign up, and to stay out of the way as much as possible, this app simply keeps track of your next reading by means of a cookie. It **ONLY** marks the month and day of the reading that you last read.
